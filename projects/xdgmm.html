Clustering with complete uncertainty

Finding co-moving groups of staras with Extreme Deconvolution Gaussian Mixture Models

  • Edited August 8 2023

Clustering is one of my favorite types of problems in machine-learning. I explore data sets to see if there structure in the data that can be used to solve a problem. In the case of Open clusters (more than 12 gravitationally bound stars), we have actual physical structures in the position measured in the night sky. We also have motions which let us consider these objects in a position-velocity phase space.
